  2. 2-The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.

  3. 3-The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.

Interesting Facts about cicada

  1. 1-Cicadas can’t eat, getting nutrients from tree roots Cicadas can’t eat or even bite, because they lack jaws.
    However, they will use a straw-like appendage to suck fluids from trees and their roots.
    Without those fluids and nutrients, the 13 and 17-year broods would surely not survive underground.

  2. 2-Some cicadas could explode with fungus.

  3. 3-Their circadian rhythms are still mysterious.
